Dollar BMW Campaign

Project Summary

Dollar’s car rental pages did not contain much imagery, copy, or enticing text. We did a full content overhaul to showcase the new BMW X3’s in the fleet and add interest to the prestige rental page.

Team Members & Collaborators

Dollar Brand Team, UX Team

Tools

Photoshop, Adobe Dreamweaver

Analysis

The Dollar website was an extremely old site hosted on legacy platforms. The IT team that still had access to the back-end systems was extremely small and mainly worked on system updates to keep the website from crashing. The CMS platform was also very old and only had access to the body html. Since the plan was to move to a new platform altogther, we did what we could from the front-end to make the content as visually pleasing as possible until we moigrated the website to the new platform.


While I did not keep a screenshot of the Prestige page before the revamp (sadly) I took a screenshot of the SUV page which was built using the exact same layout and will give you an idea of the before and after that took place.



Design & Development

Our team only had access to the body html in the CMS platform so we could not edit things like the main navigation or footer. We also could not update the body container to expand to it's full-width as these CSS rules were in a stylesheet we did not have access to. Were were however able to update the everything between the body tags, so we wanted to optimize the content area as much as possible.

Summary

In comparison with the other Collection pages, the number of transactions from the Prestige Collection page was 127% higher after the revamp than transactions on similar pages (like the Luxury Collection page). Conversion rate was also 35% higher.
