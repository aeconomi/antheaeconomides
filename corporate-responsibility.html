Corporate Responsibility

Project Summary

Our old corporate responsibility page had very little content and similar to other legacy pages, the page was not mobile-responsive. We knew we needed a section for press releases and annual reports but we also wanted to make the page inviting. I worked with a UX designer to bring the design to life.

Team Members & Collaborators

IBM Garage, UX, Loyalty, Analytics team, and Marketing Stakeholders

Tools

Photoshop, Adobe XD, Adobe Dreamweaver, Sitecore

Analysis

Hertz was expanding the scope of it's corporate responsibility programs to make a positive impact on the environment, its employees, and the sustainiability of its fleet. While the company was taking active steps towards ramping up the program, communication of these efforts was lacking. The previous landing page was very sparse, with a few links to fuel-efficient cars and three Corporate Responsibility reports.



Plan

The Hertz Corporate Responsibility team contacted the UX team member for help designing a new landing page that would better communicate Hertz's commitment towards corporate responsibility. The UX team's goal was to include different sections attributed to the program, that would be relevant to the page's primary visitors: travel conscious customers, potential employees, investors, and other proponents of corporate responsibility. My goal was to create a responsive landing page that loaded quickly and would be easy to maintain since it would receive ongoing updates as new press releases and reports were published.
