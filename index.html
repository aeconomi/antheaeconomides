Projects

Here are a few projects I've worked on during my time at Hertz and during my free time.

Hertz Digital Transformation

Hertz.com was a 2,000+ page site (6,000+ including translations) living on multiple platforms, lacking page ownership, using multiple design systems with content that was time consuming to maintain. It was time for an update. As part of the content team, I helped migrate and optimize the content moving to the new CMS platform.

Hertz Mobile App Redesign

The old Hertz app was outdated and required development in order to update content. In partnering with IBM Garage, our team worked with the Marketing, UX, and Creative Services teams to recreate the look and feel for the new Hertz Mobile App.

Hertz Corporate Responsibility

Our old corporate responsibility page had very little content and similar to other legacy pages, the page was not mobile-responsive. We knew we needed a section for press releases and annual reports but we also wanted to make the page inviting. I worked with a UX designer to bring the design to life.

Hertz Loyalty Revamp

The previous gold plus rewards sign-up page was very limited. The page was not responsive, it was hard to read, and it didn't look very enticing. We wanted to highlight the main benefit, a faster rental experience all the way from booking to drop-off. I was the front-end developer on this project.

Dollar BMW X3 Campaign

Hertz partnered with CLEAR, a biometric identification company, enabling members to verify their identities using their face instead of showing a physical ID. We needed a landing page that explained the benefits to customers.
